About iGrader Essay





IGrader Essay is an Automated Essay Grading systems platform based on AI, it is a tool that can help you grade essays in English. It is used in conjunction or even independently of a human grader. Handy for busy teachers, or to students having doubts. It needs your essay and its prompt, to give your deserved grade and some feedback. Released in 2021, by some motivated students in France, it is a friend who is never tired to help with your essays.

The tool will protect the privacy of the data provided by the user.
*The essays and the prompts will not be saved or used by the website.
